Understanding Air Quality

Comprehensive guide to AQI levels and recommendations

0 - 50

Good

Minimal Impact. Air quality is considered satisfactory, and air pollution poses little or no risk.

  • ✅ Great day for outdoor activities.
  • ✅ Open windows to ventilate your home.
51 - 100

Satisfactory

May cause minor breathing discomfort to sensitive people.

  • ⚠️ sensitive individuals should consider limiting prolonged outdoor exertion.
101 - 200

Moderate

May cause breathing discomfort to people with lungs, asthma and heart diseases.

  • ❌ Children and elderly should limit prolonged outdoor exertion.
201 - 300

Poor

May cause breathing discomfort to most people on prolonged exposure.

  • ❌ Avoid prolonged outdoor physical activities.
  • 😷 Wear an N95 mask for essential travel.
301 - 400

Very Poor

May cause respiratory illness on prolonged exposure. Pronounced effect on people with existing diseases.

  • 🚫 Avoid all outdoor physical activities.
  • 🏠 Keep windows closed and use an air purifier.
401 - 500+

Severe

Affects healthy people and seriously impacts those with existing diseases. Emergency conditions.

  • 🚫 Remain indoors and keep activity levels low.
  • 🚪 Ensure air-tightness of the house and use air filtration.
